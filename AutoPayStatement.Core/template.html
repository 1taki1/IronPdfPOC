Please check the information on this statement carefully and contact us immediately if you wish to dispute any transactions.

You will be notified if the payment fails, and will need to clear the total outstanding balance by making a manual payment either online by logging into your account, or by calling our contact centre.

If the balance remains outstanding, the service will be suspended 5 working days after the date payment was attempted, and will then be closed after a further 5 working days.

In the event your Auto Pay service is closed you will be sent a closing statement, and a final attempt will be made to collect the outstanding balance. Should this be unsuccessful, the details of any outstanding balance will eventually be shared with a Debt Recovery agency.

Once closed, you will be unable to reactivate your Auto Pay service, and will be required to start the registration process again once the total outstanding balance has been cleared.
